Privacy Policy

How we handle data for visitors and clients.

Your data

We collect only the information needed to operate and improve our site and services:

  • Website analytics: collected only if you accept cookies.
  • Contact details you provide when you email or work with us.
  • Project data shared for analyses or tooling, handled confidentially.

Usage

Data is used to deliver services, improve our products, and communicate with you about your requests. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Storage & security

We store project data securely and restrict access to the team members who need it to deliver your work. Client data is treated as confidential and is not shared without your consent.

Cookies

We use analytics cookies (Google Analytics) only if you choose to accept them. These cookies help us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it. You can decline cookies at any time using the banner at the bottom of the page.

Your choices

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time. Email info@deita.eu and we'll respond within a reasonable timeframe.
